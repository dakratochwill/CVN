Collaboration

CCI is a robust network of industry, higher education, and economic development partners across the Commonwealth of Virginia. By enhancing collaborations, CCI is building an ecosystem greater than the sum of its parts.

CCI includes teams developed across the Network that provide interdisciplinary capability and expertise in AI assurance, 5G wireless security, and cyberphysical system security.
